Service

What this is

InvoiceBridge, operated by Worqflow, is an Atlassian Forge app for Jira that creates unsent invoices in the QuickBooks Online company you connect, from Jira worklog data. It is licensed, not sold, through the Atlassian Marketplace under Atlassian's standard purchase terms.

You

Your responsibilities

  • Review every invoice in QuickBooks before sending it. The app creates unsent invoices only; amounts are computed from worklog hours rounded to two decimals at your configured rates.
  • Keep worklogs, rates, currency configuration, and your QuickBooks Company ID accurate.
  • Only connect a QuickBooks company you have the right to connect, and only use Jira data you're permitted to access.
